Company Profile

THIRD EYE INFOSOLUTIONS (TES) is a registered under the Companies Ordinance of India , extending End-to-End professional in IP-CCTV and building security solutions to companies of repute throughout India HISTORY OF THE COMPANY/BUSINESS

THIRD EYE INFOSOLUTIONS (TES) was established in 1ST JAN 2020 and started working as a Business Associate of CCTV & IP VIDEO PHONE Products DAHUA , HIKVISION, CP PLUS,ESSL, ZKTECO , HONEYWELL etc. In start we were offering only CCTV cameras system for our valued clients. By the time span, we started offering different other security products to our customers like Fire Alarm System, Access Controls, Attendance Management Solutions, Safety products, Bomb Explosive Detectors etc. Now we are leading security products distribution and installation company in India
