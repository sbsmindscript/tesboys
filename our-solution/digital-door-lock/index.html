Digital Door Lock

  • Digital Door Solutions

    Gone are the days of bulky padlocks and easily misplaced keys. The future is keyless. The digital era gives you advanced door security, eliminating the hassle of traditional locks and offering a more streamlined, safe, and modern way to protect what's important.

Features

Card Activation

Quick access using RFID cards.

PIN Entry

A simple, secure numeric code grants entry.

Bluetooth Connection

Open doors seamlessly using your mobile phone's Bluetooth.

Fingerprint Recognition

Personalized access via biometric fingerprint verification.

Traditional Key Mechanism

The outside includes traditional key access, while the inside boasts a state-of-the-art mechanical lock.

Video Entry

Connect and control access through video door phones.

Applications

  • Hotel Door Solutions

    Modern hotels opt for wire-free, keyless systems. With digital door locks, properties can manage all access points – be it guest rooms, offices, fitness centers, or parking areas. Centralized control ensures top-notch security at every corner.

  • Residential Security

    For homes, schools, and businesses, digital door locks present a practical and cost-effective alternative. Bypass the hassle of traditional keys and embrace a mechanism designed for the sheer volume of daily entries and exits. With our digital solutions, ease and security walk hand in hand.
