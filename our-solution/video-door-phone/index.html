Video Door Phone

  • Video Door Phone

    Our Video Door Phones are the new game-changers in home security. They put the power right in your hands, offering features to amp up your home's safety. Plus, you've got the added perks like intercom and access control.

Features

Guard Phone

No more scrambling for extensions. Guards can quickly find flats, keep an eye on CCTV and the lobby, and spring into action when needed. They can even open doors right from their station.

Door Camera

Compatible with a mobile app, you can view outdoor units on the go. Engage with visitors via live video, remotely unlock doors, and promptly take necessary actions.

Lobby Panel

It's got all the bells and whistles. Visitors can quickly reach the guard, and the panel's tech features, such as Face Recognition, make access smooth. Plus, the system snaps a pic whenever someone's let in to keep track.

Indoor Monitor

When someone's at the door, you'll know. Get alerts straight to your phone, store visitor images, and tap into live CCTV feeds anytime.

All-in-One Control Wizard

No more juggling different gadgets! Our indoor station is your ultimate command center, seamlessly managing all your security and alarm systems. It’s like having a smart, efficient, and super intuitive hub at your fingertips – simplifying control in a sophisticated way!

Crystal-Clear Views

Thanks to the IP network's power, enjoy super sharp HD quality. Dark or bright, you won't miss a thing with our wide viewing angle.

Touch and Talk

Who needs buttons when you've got a touchscreen? Chat with visitors, peek at live views, and buzz them in with a tap. It's like your doorman went digital – easy, breezy, and super interactive!

Smart Entry, No Swiping

Say hello to hassle-free access! Our intelligent authentication means you can walk right in without fumbling for cards. It's like having a VIP pass every time you enter – smooth, swift, and touch-free.

Camera Survelliance

Common CCTV Camera View on the monitor. Seamless integration with common CCTV setups, the Video Door Phone delivers real-time visitor footage directly to your monitor for secure and convenient entry management.
