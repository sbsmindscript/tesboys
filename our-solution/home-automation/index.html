Home Automation

  • Home Automation

    Home automation is the seamless coordination of your home’s electronic devices via the internet/Wi-Fi, offering the luxury of remote oversight. Control your lighting, music, temperature, or curtains with a simple touch on your device. Our suite of home automation and touch switch options enrich and individualize your living space.

Features

Intelligent Lighting

Transform your home’s atmosphere with customized lighting scenes that reflect your mood or the occasion with simplicity and flair.

Automated Curtain Operation

Manage your window treatments effortlessly with your smartphone. Schedule their adjustments to align with your daily habits.

Scheduled Automation

Program your lighting to switch on or off automatically, in tandem with your personalized schedule, for the ultimate convenience.

Silent Dimming

Adjust your fans to the desired speed with a quiet operation, ensuring a comfortable and serene environment.

Smart Appliance Management

Take command of your appliances, such as air conditioners and geysers, with mobile or voice commands, promoting efficient energy usage.

Scene Controller

Easily manage all your home devices with a single click from any location.

IR Blaster

Consolidate your remote controls into one IR Blaster to manage TVs, ACs, music systems, and more conveniently.

Touch Switches

Upgrade to modern touch switches for a smarter, sleeker home experience.

RGB Controller

Adjust your home's mood lighting effortlessly with the versatile RGB Controller.

Security Upgrade

Automated announcements keep people informed in emergencies, guide evacuations, or relay critical information with clarity and speed.

Geo Fencing

Automate actions with geo-fencing triggers that activate when you enter or leave designated areas

Sunrise/Sunset

Experience effortless scene-setting by syncing with nature's rhythm of sunrise/sunset.
